# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

vlaina is a desktop application designed to help you work with local notes and AI features. This policy explains, at a high level, what information the app may handle.

## Local Data

Your notes, files, workspace data, settings, and app preferences are stored locally on your device unless you choose to connect or use an online service.

vlaina does not require an account for local note editing. Files you open or create remain under your control on your device or in the folders you choose.

## AI Providers

If you configure an AI provider or use managed AI features, the content you send to the AI model may be transmitted to that provider or service so it can generate a response.

You are responsible for reviewing the privacy practices of any third-party AI provider you connect. Avoid sending sensitive information to providers you do not trust.

## Account and Billing

If you sign in, subscribe, or use managed online features, vlaina may process account information such as your email address, profile details, authentication status, membership tier, and billing-related status.

Payment processing may be handled by a third-party payment provider. vlaina does not need to store your full payment card details in the desktop app.

## Web and Media Features

If you use features that open links, resolve media URLs, fetch web content, or perform web search, the app may contact external websites or services needed to complete that action.

Those external services may receive information such as the URL requested, your IP address, and standard browser or network metadata.

## Update Checks

vlaina may check GitHub Releases to determine whether a newer desktop version is available. This sends a request to GitHub and may include standard network metadata such as your IP address and user agent.

Updates are currently offered as downloads. The app does not install updates in the background.

## Secrets and API Keys

API keys and provider secrets are intended to be stored locally by the desktop app. Treat these values as sensitive and only enter keys for providers you trust.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only when needed to provide features you choose to use, such as AI providers, authentication, billing, web search, update checks, or external links.

## Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards for the desktop app, but no software or network service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as vlaina changes. The latest version should be available in this repository.

## Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue or contact the project maintainers through the official repository:
